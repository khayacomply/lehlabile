About Lehlabile

Founded in 2020 by Sipho Nkuna, Lehlabile Funeral Services was born from a simple promise: “To ensure every family can bury their loved one with dignity — no matter their budget.”

Located at 25 Clarinet Street, Tasbet Park ext2 1043, we serve communities across Johannesburg, Soweto, and beyond — with compassion, cultural awareness, and transparency.

Our Promise to You

  • ✅ No Hidden Fees — All costs clearly listed
  • ✅ 6-Month Waiting Period — Standard for new members
  • ✅ Cash Back Policy — Get your cover amount if you don’t use our service
  • ✅ Personal Agent Support — Sipho Nkuna available 24/7 via WhatsApp

We believe in walking beside you — not just managing logistics. Our team includes trained funeral directors, community liaisons, and grief companions who understand what you’re going through.
